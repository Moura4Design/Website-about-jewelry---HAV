About HAV

HAV Jewelry is an upcoming jewelry brand based in the Netherlands. All jewelry is designed and created by the owner, Jennie Holm-Chadid. This Swedish designer mixes the cool an clean characteristics of Scandinavian design with semi-precious gemstones in colour combinations inspired by the sea and vivid color combinations found is Morocco and India. Materials used are 14k and 18k solid gold-fill or 22k and 24k vermeil.
